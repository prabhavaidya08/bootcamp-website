BOOTCAMP

Bootcamp is a self-paced coaching program where participants will be working on a
real-world project of developing a road mapping tool.

Applicants can choose either one of the project streams of , Frontend Development , Backend Development , or Project Management.

Based on the chosen stream, participants will then learn while working on the tool like a real-world project. Members organizing the Bootcamp will coordinate, coach, and provide support to participants throughout the Bootcamp.
